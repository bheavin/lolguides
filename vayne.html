Preface

This guide will assume that the reader already has knowledge over what Vayne's abilities do, and a basic understanding of what the champion does in the game. If you do not understand what Vayne does, I would recommend looking at Vayne's Wiki Page and/or playing the game more.

When Should Vayne be Picked?

Vayne's Strengths Include:

  • Tank-shredding
  • Kiting capability/movement
  • Build variability
  • Dualing ability
  • Strong scaling

Vayne's Weaknesses Include:

  • Short range
  • High economy
  • Weak laning
  • Single-target
  • Mediocre teamfighting

Vayne's Ideal Team

With these given strengths and weaknesses, we can conclude that Vayne wants to play with a team that can enable her and let her get the majority of farm from minions and jungle camps during the mid-late game. Vayne essentially wants to play with a low-economy team who can enable her to carry by buffing, healing, shielding, or peeling her.

Vayne's Biggest Counter(s)

Due to Vayne's biggest counter being range, you want to avoid picking Vayne into teams with more range than you, examples being ranged mages and enchanters such as Vel'Koz and Nami.

Another big counter to Vayne is anything that limits her movement and kiting. Movement and kiting are a huge strength to Vayne, so it is important to not pick her into champions that can limit your movement or kiting abilities such as Nasus, Veigar, Ashe, Malzahar, or Orianna.

Vayne's Botlane Synergies

Vayne is somewhat flexible when it comes to her support synergies. She can work well with engage supports such as Nautilus or Leona. She also works well with enchanters such as Yuumi, Nami, or Lulu. However, make sure to not pick Vayne when your support is a mage carry support such as Xerath or Vel'Koz. This type of support provides nothing to Vayne, and makes her laning 10x more difficult (unless they are very very good).

Vayne's Botlane Counters

As stated previously, Vayne is hard countered by range. This makes it very difficult to play her into ranged supports. She is countered by every ranged support in the game, with no exceptions. As for ADC matchups, she can work well into champions that cannot bully her for cs'ing, such as Kai'Sa, Ezreal, or Zeri. However, in a vacuum, all of Vayne's ADC matchups are playable. Vayne's matchups are highly dependent on the supports. She can do very poorly into Kai'Sa, Ezreal, or Zeri if the support matchup is skewed.
